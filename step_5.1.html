STEP 5.1:

Here are some things you can do to make your character stronger:

  • If you do something mean, apologize later
  • When you are angry, close your eyes and take three deep breaths.
  • When you are sad or upset, remember something in your life that is good and think about that thing
  • Before you speak, think about how your words will affect other people
  • When someone else succeeds, rejoice with them
  • When someone else is struggling, give them encouragement
  • When others are mean to you, turn around and walk away. Forgive them
